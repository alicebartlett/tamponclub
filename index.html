In offices that don't provide free tampons for staff, it's easy to get caught out when you need one. Tampon Club is just a bunch of women leaving tampons in their workplace toilets, so that they're available when needed. No more walking back to your locker to get out a tampon, no more sneaking one up your sleeve. Simple as that.

Can I start my own Tampon Club?

Yes! Having a Tampon Club in your office is brilliant and extremely easy to do. Head over to our page about setting one up to find out more. Once you're done, don't forget to let us know! Send us an email to hi@tampon.club or catch us on Twitter at @tampon_club. Send us a photo of your set up and maybe we'll send you some Tampon Club stickers.

Who is behind this?

A shadowy cabal of menstruating women, natch.
