Welcome to Tampon Club

In offices that don't provide free tampons for staff, it's easy to get caught out when you need one. Tampon Club is just a bunch of women leaving tampons in their workplace toilets, so that they're there when needed. No more walking back to your locker to get out a tampon, no more sneaking one up your sleeve. Simple as that.

Can I start my own Tampon Club?

Yes! Having a Tampon Club in your office is brilliant and extremely easy to do. Head over to our page about setting one up to find out more and, once you're done, drop us an email and we'll send you some Tampon Cub stickers.

Who is behind this?

A shadowy cabal of menstruating women, natch.
